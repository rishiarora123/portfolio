COPTER RESCUE

Copter Rescue is a side scrolling game made with Flash and ActionScript 3.0 in which you fly a copter avoiding obstacles. You can shoot missiles to hit the obstacles. The graphics were made in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.

THE BRAIN EXPERIMENT

The Brain Experiment is a puzzle game made with Adobe Flash and ActionScript 3.0 in which the player has to clear 16 different puzzle levels to finish the game. All graphics for this game were designed by Yogita Chawdhary.

BLINKING BLOCKS

Blinking Blocks is an arcade game made with Adobe Flash and ActionScript 3.0. The objective of the game is to make different shapes by fixing moving blocks at their correct position. The game tests your timing and reaction speed.
